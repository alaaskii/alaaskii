Open world is a type of video game level design concept, where the player can freely roam the virtual world and change any factor to his liking. This sentence describes the game environment itself and indicates the absence of some artificial barriers. Despite being called games by this name, many open-world games still impose restrictions at some points in the game environment, either due to absolute limitations in game design or temporary limitations within the game (such as locked areas) imposed by linear games. The game's levels are designed as wide open areas with many ways to reach the goal.
